## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-29

### Added
- Initial release of CarePilot Health Companion.
- FastAPI backend with SQLModel persistence.
- Next.js 14 frontend with Tailwind CSS.
- Event-driven workflow spine with LangGraph.
- Multimodal meal analysis (vision + text).
- Medication adherence tracking and reminders.
- Clinical summary and trend visualizations.
